"In a quiet, coastal town, a young woman named Elara discovers a hidden journal detailing her grandmother's tragic past. As she delves deeper into the handwritten pages, she uncovers a web of secrets, betrayals, and unrequited love that has haunted her family for generations. Elara must confront the painful truths of her family history while struggling to reconcile her own desires with the legacy she has inherited. The film explores themes of grief, forgiveness, and the enduring power of memory."
